Update and refine the "Side Hustle Tracker" web app UI and terminology.

Goal:
Make the app feel like a unique, polished finance product—not a clone of any existing app. Maintain a clean, modern dark-mode design with a money-focused aesthetic, while simplifying and refining the overall look and language.

---

TERMINOLOGY UPDATE

* Replace all instances of:
  "Side Hustle" → "Income Stream"
  "Hustles" → "Income Streams"

* The language should feel more professional and polished, like a finance tool rather than a casual app.

Examples:

* “Add Income Stream”
* “Top Income Stream”
* “Income Stream Breakdown”

---

UI STYLE REFINEMENT

1. Design Direction:

* Keep dark mode as the default (charcoal or dark gray, not pure black)
* Avoid copying Spotify or Robinhood layouts directly
* Use a more neutral, balanced design that feels like a modern finance dashboard

2. Color System:

* Primary background: dark gray/charcoal

* Text: white and light gray

* Primary accent: green (for income, progress, positive values)

* Secondary accent: subtle gold (for milestones/achievements)

* Optional: very subtle blue or purple for secondary UI elements

* Avoid heavy gradients or overly bold/neon colors

* Keep colors minimal and intentional

---

LAYOUT & STRUCTURE

* Maintain a clean, spaced-out layout with strong visual hierarchy
* Avoid large card stacks that resemble other apps
* Use simple sections with clear separation:

Dashboard structure:

1. Top: Total Income (large, bold, centered or left-aligned)
2. Below: Goal progress bar
3. Middle: Income Stream breakdown (simple list or bars)
4. Bottom: Recent transactions

* Keep max 3 main sections/screens

---

TYPOGRAPHY & SPACING

* Use clean, modern fonts
* Large numbers for key metrics
* Generous spacing between elements
* Avoid cramped layouts

---

BUTTONS & INTERACTIONS

* Keep a floating or fixed “+ Add Income” button
* Buttons should be simple (rounded, minimal styling)
* Avoid flashy or oversized UI elements

---

MOTIVATION & GAMIFICATION (REFINED)

* Keep streaks, goals, and milestones
* Make them feel subtle and mature (not playful or cartoonish)
* Use minimal icons (e.g., small flame for streaks, small trophy for milestones)
* Highlight achievements with gold accents (not bright colors)

---

INSIGHTS DISPLAY

* Keep insights short and clean (1 line each)

* Example:
  “Your top income stream is Reselling”
  “You earned 20% more this week”

* Avoid cluttering the dashboard with too many stats

---

OVERALL FEEL

The app should feel:

* Clean
* Fast
* Focused
* Slightly premium
* More like a personal finance tool than a social or entertainment app

---

STRICT RULES

* Do NOT copy layouts from Spotify or Robinhood
* Do NOT add unnecessary UI elements
* Do NOT overuse color or animations
* Prioritize clarity, simplicity, and usability

---

PRIORITY

1. Clean, unique UI
2. Clear terminology (Income Streams)
3. Maintain fast user experience
4. Keep motivation features subtle
